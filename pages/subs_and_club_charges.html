Subscriptions & How to Sign Up

Annual Club Member Cost:

$480.00

Subs are due in October each year. We would appreciate payment by online banking

Membership rate for *new members only starting during the year are as below. [*new = never having rowed for Union previously].

  • 1st Oct to 31st Dec-Full rate $480 incl GST
  • 1st Jan to 31st Mar-3/4 rate $360 incl GST
  • 1st April to 30th June-1/2 rate $240 incl GST
  • 1st July to end of year-1/4 rate $120 incl GST

Followed by a full annual fee due from 1st October

Please print, complete, sign, scan and email the Application for Membership form to urcclubcaptain@gmail.com.

Saftey

Safety is everyone's responsibility at Union. It's important for all of our rowing community including rowers, coxswains and coaches to be safety conscious on and off the water.

Please be familiar with the safety protocols at our club. Information is available from the links oppisite and is also displayed in our shed.

Any Rower wanting to join union, or any current rowers who have not done so must download this swim test form, then complete the swim test and return the form before participating in any on-the-water activities with the Union Rowing Club.

All members must complete the swim test and prove they are able to swim 50 metres in light clothing and tread water for 5 minutes.

Boat racking

Union would like to offer a boat rack to each individual member who owns their own boat as part of their membership and will continue to do so as long as space allows, additional members' boats [ie: members with two or more boats] will only be considered if space allows after one boat per member has been allocated.
